# Emotecraft Wiki ## Downloading Custom Emotes Currently you can download emotes either from the old [emote archive](https://github.com/KosmX/Emotecraft-emotes) or from the #emote-list in the [Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/PSW2t4Ujm6) In the emote archive find the emote you want (eg. https://github.com/KosmX/Emotecraft-emotes/blob/master/emotes/Twerk.json). Then click the "Raw" button. Finally Right Click -> Save as. In the Discord server find the emote you want and click the download button. Next see [Installing Custom Emotes](./install-emotes) for how to install them from there.
