# Emotecraft Wiki ## Playing Emotes To play an emote simply press 'b' (or whatever you set the open emote wheel keybind too)\ Then you will see the emote wheel (See [Setting the emote wheel](/setting-emote-wheel)),\ you can select an emote from here (if you've set it up) or click "All Emotes" to view a list of all your emotes.\ To play an emote simply left click it.
